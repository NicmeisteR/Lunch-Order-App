Why

At a company I worked we always orderd lunch on fridays, however this was always done manually by one of the financial department employees.

They had to manually collect all of the orders via email and then create a list to go place the orders.

I thought to myself, this is a very uneffiecnt way of doing things. So I did a quick brainstorm and came up with this app.

How

This app is built with my favorite front end framework Angular, I handled the database with Firebase from Google as its super easy to implement and requires no additional servers or anything.
